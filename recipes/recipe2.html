Beer Can Chicken

Juicy Tender Chicken thats cooking literally on top of a can of your choice! Super easy too cook!

Beer Can Chicken Recipe

  • 1 Whole Chicken
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 12oz beer, room temp, opened & half full
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper

Cooking steps

  1. Preheat Grill to 450 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Season chicken and rub with olive oil
  3. Place chicken onto the beercan or can of choice
  4. Grill on direct heat, check every 15min until internal temp is 165 degrees fahrenheit
  5. Lastly enjoy!
