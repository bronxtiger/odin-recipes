Easy Chicken Pot Pie Recipe

  • 1 can (10 1/2oz) Campbell's cream of chicken soup
  • 1 cup of reduced fat (2%) milk
  • 1 cup cubed cooked chicken or turkey
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup of biscuit baking mix

Cooking Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Stir the soup, 1/2 cup of milk, veggies of choice, chicken, and seasoning into ovenproof skillet.
  3. Stir remaining ingredients into a small bowl and lather chicken/turkey
  4. Bake for 20 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
